Ingredients for a sandwich

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • Cheddar
    • Mozzarella
    • Pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Recipe

  1. Spreaad butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in fring pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
